The signature star map pin — every attraction is a star on the night map. Color by category; `selected` enlarges it and lights the glow.

```jsx
<MapPin color="var(--accent)" label="Praia da Pipa" selected />
<MapPin color="var(--aurora)" twinkle />
```

Props: `color`, `selected`, `size`, `label`, `twinkle`. Category colors: praias → `--info`, fortes → `--aurora`, dunas → `--star`, natureza → `--mare`.
